welcome to rl-dart
rl-dart is a rocket league directional air roll trainer. use it to practice and visualize air roll mechanics, car control, and ball interaction in a 3d environment.
controls
  • keyboard: w/s = pitch, a/d = yaw, q/e = air roll
  • controller: left stick = pitch/yaw, X = air roll left, Y = air roll right
